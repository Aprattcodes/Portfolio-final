About Alyssa--

Alyssa Hardwick is an aspiring web developer/designer, with interest in digital and multi-media arts. After graduating with a dual BA in Studio Art and Art History from the University of Colorado Boulder, she realized the creative process of developing apps and websites. With her artistic background, there is an authenticity in her frontend development style. Her wittyness proves that she worked as a full-time bartender for almost a decade. She loves to hustle, but on a relaxing day, Alyssa enjoys spending time in her garden, walking in Tower Grove Park with her Shiba Inu Kiki, or eating a delicious meal! She is an easy going type of gal and loved by most. Bright colors, contrasting elements, and pop surrealism is her jam!
